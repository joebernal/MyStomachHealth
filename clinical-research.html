Clinical Pilot Study

We are looking to recruit 250 participants in the greater LA area to evaluate for risk for stomach cancer.

All patients will undergo risk assessment questionnaire, amongst the 250, those with at least 3 risk factors for stomach cancer plus or minus symptoms associated with stomach cancer will be offered upper endoscopy (at cost to research study or billed to insurance if indications exist).

All participants will have the opportunity to meet and discuss their risk with an expert clinician (Dr. Woo, Dr. Lin, Dr. Idos, Dr. Uradomo).

All participants will be offered free H. pylori breathalyzer testing and results review.
